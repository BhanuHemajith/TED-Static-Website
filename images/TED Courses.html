A learning experience that’s
uniquely TED.

TED’s global community connects millions of open-minded people who seek a deeper understanding of themselves, each other and the world we live in. That’s what you’ll get with TED Courses, too.

TED’s social learning approach means that you’ll be part of a group that moves through course content together, learning from your teacher and from each other.

During each week of your month-long course, we’ll unlock a new session. You’ll watch teaching segments and work through activities at your own pace, coming together with your classmates to share your questions, reactions and ideas. Virtual live events offer additional opportunities for you to create stronger connections and engage in real time with your teacher.
